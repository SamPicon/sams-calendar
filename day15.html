April 19-21

Another weekend, another adventure! This time, I’m taking a break from the solitude of the forest and mountains and heading south to San Diego. While here, I put my stealth camping skills to the test, greeted some seagulls, and explored the coast.
Pacific Beach, La Jolla, and the Cabrillo National Monument were incredible to see in person. I absolutely love it here and can definitely see myself living and working in this area. I can’t wait to come back!
