Today, I’m chasing greatness! My main goal while exploring Guadalupe Mountains National Park was simple—reach the peak. With my bag packed full of snacks, water, warm clothing (just in case), and electrolyte candies, I set off. After three hours, 300 flights of stairs’ worth of climbing, and two empty water bottles, I finally made it. My reward? An amazing view… and two fresh blisters.
