March 24th - 29th

Today, I woke up and decided to cross three state lines, each with its own time zone. First, I went from New Mexico into Texas, then back into New Mexico, and finally all the way to Arizona, where I’ll probably spend the rest of the week. Six hours of driving flew by, but now I’m absolutely exhausted. My knee hurts, and so does my sunburnt skin.
Over the next couple of days, I stayed in Tucson, soaking up the sun, revisiting some of my old stomping grounds, and spending time with friends.
My next adventure will be toward Joshua Tree National Park in California.
